The Problem

Coverage % is a lie your CI pipeline tells you

80% line coverage sounds great — until a critical checkout flow breaks in production because it was never touched by a test. Your suite covers files, not risk.

🔍

You don't know what's missing

Coverage reports show what's tested, not what matters. There's no map from "untested file" to "user impact."

🔥

Bugs surface in production first

The gap only becomes visible when customers report it. By then the cost — in trust and sprint time — is already paid.

📊

No prioritisation signal

Even teams that audit coverage manually don't have a way to rank which gaps to close first. Everything feels equal.

How It Works

From repo URL to risk report in seconds

1

Connect your GitHub repo

Paste a repo URL. We read your Playwright spec files and your source files — no install, no CI change.

2

We map tests → source files

Every route, component, and module is cross-referenced against your test suite to find what's covered and what's dark.

3

Risk-ranked gap report

Uncovered files are weighted by change frequency and page importance to surface what actually needs a test written next.

4

AI-suggested test cases

For the highest-risk gaps, get suggested test titles and scenarios ready to drop into your spec files.
